Lijiang Cable Car Tickets: Children’s Pricing Guide

Nestled in the heart of Yunnan Province, Lijiang is a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its ancient town, stunning landscapes, and vibrant Naxi culture. One of the must-do activities for visitors is taking the cable car up to Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (Yulong Xueshan), where breathtaking views and alpine adventures await. But if you’re traveling with kids, figuring out the cable car ticket pricing can be a bit confusing. This guide breaks down everything you need to know about Lijiang cable car tickets for children, including discounts, age requirements, and insider tips to make your family trip unforgettable.

Understanding Lijiang Cable Car Routes

Before diving into pricing, it’s essential to know which cable car route suits your family best. Jade Dragon Snow Mountain offers three main cable car options, each catering to different interests and fitness levels.

1. Glacier Park Cable Car (Big Cable Car)

The most popular option, this cable car takes you to an elevation of 4,506 meters, offering direct access to the glacier park. It’s a thrilling ride with panoramic views, but the high altitude means it’s not recommended for very young children or those prone to altitude sickness.

2. Spruce Meadow Cable Car (Medium Cable Car)

A gentler alternative, this route stops at 3,240 meters and leads to a serene meadow surrounded by ancient spruce trees. It’s ideal for families with younger kids or those who prefer a less intense high-altitude experience.

3. Yak Meadow Cable Car (Small Cable Car)

The most relaxed option, this cable car ascends to 3,700 meters and opens up to a vast grassland where yaks graze. It’s perfect for families looking for a scenic but low-key adventure.

Children’s Pricing for Lijiang Cable Cars

Ticket prices for children vary depending on age, height, and the specific cable car route. Here’s a detailed breakdown:

Age and Height Requirements

  • Free for infants: Children under 1.2 meters (3.9 feet) tall typically ride for free but must be accompanied by a paying adult.
  • Discounted for kids: Children between 1.2 meters and 1.4 meters (3.9–4.6 feet) tall qualify for a 50% discount on most cable car tickets.
  • Full price for older kids: Those over 1.4 meters (4.6 feet) or aged 12 and above usually pay the adult rate.

Cable Car-Specific Pricing

  1. Glacier Park Cable Car

    • Adult ticket: ¥180
    • Child ticket (1.2m–1.4m): ¥90
    • Infants (under 1.2m): Free

  2. Spruce Meadow Cable Car

    • Adult ticket: ¥60
    • Child ticket (1.2m–1.4m): ¥30
    • Infants (under 1.2m): Free

  3. Yak Meadow Cable Car

    • Adult ticket: ¥45
    • Child ticket (1.2m–1.4m): ¥22.5
    • Infants (under 1.2m): Free

Note: Prices are subject to change, especially during peak seasons like Chinese New Year or summer holidays.

Tips for Families Taking the Cable Car

Altitude Considerations

Jade Dragon Snow Mountain’s high elevation can be challenging for little ones. To avoid altitude sickness:
- Ascend gradually and take breaks.
- Stay hydrated and avoid strenuous activity.
- Consider spending a day in Lijiang (2,400 meters) to acclimatize before heading up.

Best Time to Visit

  • Spring (March–May): Mild weather and fewer crowds.
  • Autumn (September–November): Clear skies and vibrant foliage.
  • Avoid winter if traveling with very young kids, as temperatures drop drastically at higher elevations.

What to Pack

  • Layers: Temperatures fluctuate dramatically between the base and summit.
  • Sun protection: The high-altitude sun is intense.
  • Snacks: Keep energy levels up during the adventure.

Combining Cable Car Tickets with Other Attractions

Many families opt for combo tickets that include access to other Jade Dragon Snow Mountain attractions, such as:
- Impression Lijiang Show: A cultural performance directed by Zhang Yimou.
- Blue Moon Valley: A stunning turquoise lake perfect for photos.
- Ganhaizi Meadow: A scenic spot at the mountain’s base.

Combo tickets often offer savings, so check with your hotel or tour operator for bundled deals.

Booking Strategies to Save Money

  1. Buy Online in Advance: Platforms like Ctrip or Meituan often have discounts.
  2. Travel Off-Peak: Weekdays and non-holiday periods mean lower prices and smaller crowds.
  3. Group Discounts: If traveling with another family, inquire about group rates.

Final Thoughts

Exploring Jade Dragon Snow Mountain via cable car is a highlight of any Lijiang trip, and with the right planning, it can be a magical experience for kids and adults alike. By understanding the pricing structure, preparing for the altitude, and choosing the best route for your family, you’ll set the stage for an unforgettable adventure in one of China’s most breathtaking destinations.
